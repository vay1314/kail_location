可以先在下方找一找有没有自己手机的

To obtain the corresponding sensor parameters for your phone,
send the following two files to your email, issues, or Telegram: `/system/lib64/libsensor.so` and `/system/lib64/libsensorservice.so`. 
Remember to include your phone model and system version.
Wait for an update. Do not enter incorrect information, or it will restart. 
You can check below to see if your phone model is listed.
